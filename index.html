AI SMS Chatbot Development Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the development of an AI-powered SMS chatbot with phone-based authentication and subscription payments. The project spans 6-10 weeks across four phases: infrastructure setup with GTalk SMS integration and GPU deployment, core chat features including conversation management and AI implementation, payment integration using Polar's platform, and final testing/deployment. The system is designed to handle thousands of users through efficient GPU utilization and tiered pricing (Basic: 100 messages at $10/month, Standard: 500 messages at $25/month, and Premium: unlimited messages at $50/month), with costs ranging from $1.18 to $4.20 per user depending on scale. The chatbot features customizable personality traits and employs cost optimization strategies to maintain 80%+ profit margins while delivering sub-5-second response times. Monthly profit per user by tier: Basic tier generates $9.16 profit ($10 revenue - $0.84 cost), Standard tier generates $20.80 profit ($25 revenue - $4.20 cost), and Premium tier generates $41.60 profit ($50 revenue - $8.40 cost). Initial launch requires $2,444 ($465 in one-time setup costs plus $1,979 for first month operations including single A100 GPU instance), supporting approximately 250 users and reaching break-even with 80 Standard tier users.
